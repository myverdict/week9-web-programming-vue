What happened?

If you are typing code and you use a variable called 'response' without defining it, (or your definition has a typo) then VSCode will assume that you mean a variable called 'response' in the Express framework and helpfully add that import.

Unfortunately, that is not what you want to do - the correct fix is to define your response variable or correct your spelling.
